ReadQuest is an interactive platform designed for grade 11 students to engage with classic and contemporary literature through reading, games, and study resources. We make literature fun and accessible.

ReadQuest offers both free and premium content. Basic library access and many interactive games are free, while our premium subscription unlocks additional resources and game content.

Each book has companion games designed to reinforce key themes and character development. Games range from story-based adventures to trivia challenges, making learning fun as you progress through your reading. Games include comprehension quizzes, character matching, plot puzzles, and vocabulary challenges tailored to the book you're reading.
